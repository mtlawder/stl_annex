By the Numbers: Methodolgy

Updated:9/27/2019 - M. Lawder
The data used for these posts is sourced from a couple of different databases and tables obtained from the Beraeu of Transportation website. The tables used include:
  • T-100 Domestic Segment (All Carriers)
  • DB1B Coupon
  • DB1B Market
The T-100 data is used to calculate the departures, the passenger totals and the load factors. The DB1B Coupon Data is used to map regional carrier flights to their mainline airlines based on the ticketing airline. The DB1B Market data is used for all of the O&D calculations. Both DB1B tables are only use 10% of all airline ticket and therefore are only an estimation and info for based on these numbers contains a small margin of error.
